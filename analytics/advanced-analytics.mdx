## Advanced Performance Metrics

**What it is:** Extended performance statistics focused on trade quality and costs.

### Metrics Included
- Total Gain / Loss
- Total Trade Volume
- Largest Gain
- Largest Loss
- Average Win vs. Average Loss
- Average Fee per Trade

### Key Insights
- Evaluate trade quality beyond win rate
- Assess whether winners outweigh losers
- Monitor execution efficiency

## Long vs. Short Analysis

**What it is:** Directional performance comparison.

**What it's for:** Identifies whether long or short trades perform better.

### Insights Provided
- Win rate comparison
- PnL comparison
- Duration differences

### Key Insights
- Discover directional edge
- Optimize bias based on market conditions

## Day of Week Analysis

**What it is:** PnL and activity breakdown by weekday.

**What it's for:** Identifies recurring weekly performance patterns.

### Key Insights
- Optimize trading schedule
- Avoid consistently underperforming days

## Time of Day Analysis

**What it is:** Hourly performance breakdown.

**What it's for:** Identifies peak profitability hours.

### Key Insights
- Align trading activity with optimal market windows
- Especially useful for day traders and scalpers

## Trading Session Analysis

**What it is:** Performance grouped by trading sessions.

**What it's for:** Evaluates endurance, discipline, and session quality.

### Key Insights
- Detect overtrading
- Identify optimal session length
