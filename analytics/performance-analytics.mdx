## PnL Chart

A cumulative equity curve visualizes long-term performance progression and volatility.

### How to use
- View equity growth over time
- Hover for exact values
- Fully respects active filters
- Toggle between PnL and Drawdown views

### Key Insights
- Identify strong and weak performance phases
- Evaluate consistency versus volatility

## Drawdown Chart

A visualization of peak-to-trough losses measures downside risk and recovery behavior.

### How to use
- View percentage drawdown from historical peaks
- Identify maximum drawdown periods
- Switch via chart mode selector

### Key Insights
- Understand worst-case scenarios
- Evaluate whether risk aligns with your tolerance

## Symbol & Date Filters

Interactive filters enable focused analysis of specific assets, strategies, or market conditions by narrowing data by trading pair and time range.

### How to use
- **Symbol Filter:** Select individual symbols or "All Symbols"
- **Date Range:** Choose custom start and end dates

### Data Availability
- **Jupiter:** May 1st, 2025 onward (protocol data availability)
- **Backpack:** Full historical range based on your activity

### Key Insights

- Identify which markets and periods suit your strategy best
