## Trade Duration Analysis

**What it is:** Distribution of holding periods across time ranges.

### Duration Buckets
- Seconds
- Minutes
- Hours
- Days
- Weeks

### Key Insights
- Understand your trading style
- Identify optimal holding periods
- Detect premature exits or overstaying positions

## Order Type Analysis

**What it is:** Performance comparison by order type.

**What it's for:** Optimize execution strategy.

### Key Insights
- Identify whether market or limit orders perform better
- Understand slippage and fill quality impact

## Stop Loss Analysis

**What it is:** Analysis of stop-loss usage effectiveness.

**What it's for:** Evaluate risk management discipline.

### Key Insights
- Determine if stop losses improve performance
- Optimize placement and usage
