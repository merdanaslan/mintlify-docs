## Performance Metric Cards

**What it is:** A set of five high-level performance metrics displayed prominently at the top of the Overview tab.

**What it's for:** Provides an immediate snapshot of your overall trading activity and performance without requiring deeper analysis.

### Metrics Included

- **Total PnL** — Gross profit or loss before fees (green for profit, red for loss)
- **Total Volume** — Cumulative traded notional across all positions
- **Total Fees Paid** — Aggregate fees across all trades
- **Total Trades** — Count of all trades (open, closed, liquidated)
- **First Trade Date** — Timestamp of your first recorded trade

### Key Insights

- Quickly assess overall profitability and activity level
- Understand cost impact relative to volume
- Establish historical context for your trading activity
