## Time Period Performance

**What it is:** Daily, weekly, and monthly performance breakdowns.

**What it's for:** Identifies consistency and temporal performance patterns.

### How to use
- Review PnL and trade count per time bucket
- Spot profitable and unprofitable periods

### Key Insights
- Detect seasonality and streak behavior
- Understand performance concentration
