---
title: "Who It's For"
description: "Golden Pocket is designed for traders of all styles and experience levels"
---

Golden Pocket is built for anyone who wants to improve their trading performance.

It is suitable for all trading styles and approaches, including:
- Discretionary traders
- Swing traders
- Day traders
- Scalpers
- Algorithmic traders
- Market makers
- Proprietary traders

Whether trading alone or as part of a team, Golden Pocket provides a structured way to review performance, understand behavior, and make data-driven improvements over time.
