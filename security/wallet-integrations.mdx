---
title: "Wallet-Based Integrations"
description: "How Golden Pocket connects to decentralized trading venues"
---

For decentralized perpetual trading venues, Golden Pocket connects by reading publicly available on-chain data associated with a wallet address.

## What's Required
- Only your wallet address

## What's NOT Required
- Private keys
- Signing permissions  
- Transaction approvals

<Note>
No sensitive wallet information is ever needed or stored.
</Note>
