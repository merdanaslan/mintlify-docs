Golden Pocket is designed with professional use cases in mind, including individual traders, trading teams, and companies.

## Professional Standards

The platform emphasizes:
- **Clear separation** between analytics and execution
- **Transparent** data access boundaries
- **Predictable** and auditable behavior

## Suitable Environments

This makes Golden Pocket suitable for use in professional and institutional environments where data integrity and operational safety are critical.
