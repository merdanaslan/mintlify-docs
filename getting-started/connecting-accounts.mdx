Golden Pocket connects to trading venues in a read-only manner.

Depending on the platform, this may involve:
- **Providing a wallet address** 
- **Providing read-only API credentials**

<Warning>
These connections are used solely to read trading data. Golden Pocket cannot place trades, modify orders, or access funds.
</Warning>
